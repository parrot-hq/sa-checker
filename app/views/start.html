Check if you need to send a Self Assessment tax return

Use this service to find out if you need to complete a Self Assessment tax return for the 2023 to 2024 tax year (6 April 2023 to 5 April 2024).

You may need to send a return if you:

  • were self-employed as a sole trader and earned more than £1,000
  • were a partner in a business partnership
  • had income from savings, investments or dividends
  • had a total taxable income of more than £100,000
  • received Child Benefit and you or your partner had income over £50,000
  • had income from abroad
  • sold or disposed of assets and need to pay Capital Gains Tax

This tool takes around 5 minutes to complete.

Before you start

You'll need details of your income for the 2023 to 2024 tax year, including any income from self-employment, savings, investments or abroad.

If you already know you need to send a Self Assessment return, you can sign in to your HMRC online account to file it.

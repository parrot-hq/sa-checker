What to do next

The deadline for sending your 2023 to 2024 tax return online is 31 January 2025.

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "If you miss the deadline, you may be charged a late filing penalty of £100.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

If you have not already registered for Self Assessment, you must do this first. It can take up to 20 working days to receive your Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR).

  • Register for Self Assessment if this is your first return
  • Sign in and file your return if you are already registered
